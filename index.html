Dan is a Professor of Oceanography at Dalhousie University in Halifax, Nova Scotia, Canada. He teaches there, and is also the Graduate Coordinator. His research focus is on ocean mixing caused by internal waves and double-diffusive convection.

An official website at Dalhousie University provides contact information and (by university mandate) mentions just five publications. Colleagues or potential Honours, MSc or PhD students may wish to consult Publications tab to get a full publication list, including PDF links. The Software tab may be of interest to those who are interested in Dan's contributions to Oceanographic software. The blog tab provides Dan's blog, mostly related to Oceanographic analysis in R (the topic of his upcoming textbook).
